## nop-ai-agent Middleware 洋葱链 + Tool Tag System 实现 (Plan 296)

**Plan**: `ai-dev/plans/296-nop-ai-agent-middleware-and-tool-tag-system-implementation.md`

### Workstream 1 — Middleware 洋葱链

- 新建 `io.nop.ai.agent.middleware` 包：`IAgentMiddleware` 接口 + `MiddlewareChain` 不可变类
- `IHookRegistry` 新增 `getMiddlewares()` / `registerMiddleware()` default 方法；`NoOpHookRegistry` 覆盖实现（getMiddlewares 返空、register 抛异常）；`DefaultHookRegistry` 新增 middleware 存储 + `buildChain()` 方法
